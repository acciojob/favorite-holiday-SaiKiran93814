Munnar


Munnar is a breathtaking hill station in Kerala, India, known for its lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and cool climate. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it is a paradise for nature lovers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers.

    Top 5 attractions

  1. Tea Gardens & Tata Tea Museum
  2. Eravikulam National Park
  3. Mattupetty Dam & Lake
  4. Attukal Waterfalls
  5. Top Station

    Must-try foods

  • Kerala Sadya (Traditional Feast)
  • Appam with Stew
  • Malabar Parotta with Kerala-style Curry
  • Kerala-style Prawn or Fish Curry
  • Cardamom Tea & Banana Fritters (Pazham Pori)
